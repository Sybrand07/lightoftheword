Jacob's Life Events & Estimated Ages

Event Estimated Age Scriptural Basis
Jacob & Esau are born 0 years old Genesis 25:26 – Isaac was 60 when Jacob and Esau were born.
Jacob gets Esau’s birthright ~15 years old Genesis 25:29-34 – Occurs before Isaac's blessing; Esau was old enough to hunt.
Jacob steals Esau’s blessing ~77 years old Genesis 27 – This occurs just before Jacob flees to Padan-Aram.
Jacob flees to Haran 77 years old Genesis 28 – Isaac sends Jacob away to avoid Esau’s wrath and find a wife.
Jacob serves Laban for Leah & Rachel 84 years old Genesis 29:18-28 – Works 7 years before marrying Leah and Rachel.
Birth of Joseph ~91 years old Genesis 30:25 – Joseph is born before Jacob leaves Laban; occurs after 14 years of work (7 for Leah & Rachel, 7 for livestock).
Jacob leaves Laban & returns to Canaan 97 years old Genesis 31 – He serves Laban for 6 more years after Joseph’s birth (Genesis 31:41).
Jacob wrestles with God (name changed to Israel) ~97 years old Genesis 32 – Happens before meeting Esau.
Jacob reunites with Esau 97 years old Genesis 33 – Takes place after 20 years in Haran.
Death of Rachel (birth of Benjamin) ~104 years old Genesis 35:16-19 – Occurs shortly after leaving Laban.
Joseph sold into slavery ~108 years old Genesis 37:2 – Joseph was 17 years old at this time.
Jacob moves to Egypt (famine years) 130 years old Genesis 47:9 – Jacob tells Pharaoh he is 130.
Jacob blesses Ephraim & Manasseh ~147 years old Genesis 48 – Before his death.
Death of Jacob 147 years old Genesis 49:33 – Dies in Egypt.

Key Takeaways

  • Jacob was 77 when he fled to Haran, much older than often depicted.
  • He worked for Laban for 20 years (14 for Leah & Rachel, 6 for livestock).
  • He was 130 when he arrived in Egypt during the famine.
  • He lived 17 years in Egypt and died at 147.
