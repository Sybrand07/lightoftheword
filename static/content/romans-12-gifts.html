Romans 12: Gifts that are Practical

Romans 12:6-8 (with explanations):
"Having gifts that differ according to the grace given to us, let us use them: if prophecy (the ability to speak God's truth or reveal His will), in proportion to our faith; if service (helping others through practical acts of assistance), in our serving; the one who teaches (instructing and explaining God's Word), in his teaching; the one who exhorts (encouraging, comforting, or motivating others to live faithfully), in his exhortation; the one who contributes (giving generously of resources to meet needs), in generosity; the one who leads (providing direction and oversight), with zeal; the one who does acts of mercy (showing compassion to the suffering or marginalized), with cheerfulness."
